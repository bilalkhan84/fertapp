## TYPEFORM AI PROMPT (paste this)

Create a feedback form for FertTrack, a male fertility tracking app currently in beta. The audience is men aged 30–50 who are navigating the fertility journey. The form should feel warm and non-clinical — not like a medical intake, more like a conversation.

The goal is to understand who our users are, what their current situation looks like, what resources they rely on today, and how FertTrack fits into that. Keep it to 15 questions maximum.

Cover these areas in this order:

1. Basic context — age range, relationship status, province/location (Ontario focus)
2. Their fertility situation — how long they've been on this journey, whether they've had a semen analysis, whether they've seen a specialist
3. Resources and information — where they currently go for information about male fertility, what tools or apps they use, who they talk to about it
4. Their experience with FertTrack — which features they've used, what's been most useful, what's missing
5. One open feedback question — anything else they want us to know
6. Optional email for follow-up

Use a mix of multiple choice, opinion scales, and short open-text questions. Keep the language conversational and encouraging. No medical jargon. End with a warm thank-you screen that mentions we read every response personally.
